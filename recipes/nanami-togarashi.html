Nanami Togarashi

Description:

Nanami togarashi is similar to Shichimi togarashi, but is distinct in the predominance of the dried citrus peel from orange, tangerine, or yuzu.

Ingredients:
  • Gochugaru
  • Powdered dehydrated tangerine zest
  • Toasted white sesame seeds
  • Black sesame seeds
  • Ground Sancho pepper
  • Hemp hearts
  • Ground ginger
  • Toasted nori flakes
Steps:
  1. Combine all ingredients.
