Tonnato

Description:

Tonnato is an Italian condiment often made with tuna, anchovies, lemon juice, olive oil, and mayonnaise.

Ingredients:
  • Skipjack tuna, packed in oil
  • Anchovy, salt-packed and cleaned
  • Egg yolk
  • Lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • White pepper, freshly cracked
  • Sea salt
  • Mayonnaise, preferably kewpie
  • Olive oil
Steps:
  1. Combine first 7 ingredients into a food processor and blended until smooth
  2. With motor running, drizzle in olive oil to thin slightly
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning
