Spicy Beefy Carrots

Description:

These carrots are first rubbed with beef base & gochujiang before being cooked sous vide. They are finished under the broiler.

Ingredients:
  • Peeled whole carrots
  • Better Than Bouillon Roast Beef Base
  • Gochujiang
  • Butter knob
Steps:
  1. Preheat water bath to 183°F
  2. Combine beef base and gochujiang in equal parts
  3. Thoroughly rub all over carrots, add knob of butter, and seal in bag
  4. Cook for 1 hour
  5. Preheat broiler
  6. Cut open bag, pour liquid into saucepan to reduce to glaze
  7. Arrange carrots on a foil-lined sheetpan and roast until slightly charred
  8. Finish carrots with fully reduced glaze and serve immediately
